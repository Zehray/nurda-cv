ABOUT ME

Hello there!

I’m Nurda :) I’m a Graphic Design (BA) student at Yildiz Technical University in Istanbul and my main passion is creating Concept Art for video games and animations.

A little Background

I've been drawing as long as I can remember just like every other artist. I really liked the idea of telling stories with drawings such as comics and caricatures but at that time I didn’t know this could be also a career path.

I always wanted to make games. So I initially tried to learned programming. But when I was 16 I realized I enjoyed drawing more than typing. I also found out, is a thing called concept art and with it I can work on both games and animation which is also something I am really interested in. After deciding the way I wanna go, I found a studio and took art fundamental classes until I got accepted by Yildiz Technical University.

Present Day

Currently, I’m spending most of my time on schoolwork. I’ve done some freelance work during quarantine. In my third year I spent most of my time learning animation principles. In 2022 my friends and I started an animation project that's still ongoing.

I have been working on world-building and character concepts for a Dungeons & Dragons game I have been illustrating the unfortunate experiences of our characters.

Playing table-top RPG and board games only increased my interest on developing games.I learned about game settings and I am a game master in the Fate system :)

My toolbelt

I’m using both photoshop and procreate for my drawings. I’m also using Blender for grease pencil tool. I’m quite familiar with 3d modeling but it’s not as good as grease pencil.

I know after effects and premiere basics for editing. I’d like to create 2D reference videos before starting animation with those programs.
